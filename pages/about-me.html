About Me

What Do I Do?

My title is "Senior Test Engineer." What that means is that I create automation scripts for my clients in order to test their product. I ensure that my client's software runs silky smooth for users to enjoy. Some other things I do are code tools to gather data for other testers and manually execute test cases.

My Goals?

Being a Test Engineer for 2+ years has been extremely educational and rewarding, but I think I am ready to delve into the development world. I've always had a knack for design, so I would love to pursue a Front-End or Full-Stack Development role. Creating user-friendly UIs and designing highly-functional systems is something that I was always interested in. So, I believe my future lies in Front-End or Full-Stack Development.
